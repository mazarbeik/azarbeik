About

I am an AI researcher specializing in clinical decision support systems and deep reinforcement learning. My current work explores offline policy evaluation, Distribution Correction Estimation, and DDP-DQN architectures to optimize medical interventions in critical care environments.


I hold an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology and am currently completing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Vienna University of Technology (TU Wien). My research bridges theoretical reinforcement learning and practical, life-saving clinical applications.
