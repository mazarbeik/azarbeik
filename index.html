About

I am a researcher working on machine learning with a focus on reinforcement learning, clinical decision support in intensive care, and large language models.

My work aims to bridge the gap between theoretical advances in sequential decision-making and their deployment in high-stakes clinical settings — developing policies that assist clinicians in managing sepsis treatment, renal replacement therapy timing, and beyond.

I hold an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology and currently pursue a Ph.D. in Computer Science (Informatics) at Vienna University of Technology (TU Wien).
